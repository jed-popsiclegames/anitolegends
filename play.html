Anito Legends is currently in beta phase. We are gradually opening up the game for our community to try. Please use this time to stress test, hunt bugs, and make recommendations for our team to consider.

Please also note that the NFT's that will be airdropped to your wallets during this phase are running on Testnet and are therefore not final.
